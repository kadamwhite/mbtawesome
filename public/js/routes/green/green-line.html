Green Line

♥

The green line is awesome! Every day over two hundred thousand people board Green Line trains, traveling along routes that in parts date back over a hundred and fifty years.

It’s the best way to reach Boston landmarks like Fenway Park, or the Museum of Fine Art. In the next few years the Green Line will be extended into Somerville and Medford, the first line expansion in decades.

Real-time… in time

We don't have real-time predictions for the Green Line yet, but the MBTA is working on it. (Unfortunately, a century and a half of moving people around didn't leave the Green Line much time for API development—it’s got places to be!)
